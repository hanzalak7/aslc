ABOUT ASHOKDADA SABLE LAW COLLEGE


One of the institutes run by Mangaon shikshan prasarak mandal is named as Ashokdada sable Law College named after veteran , social worker, great thinker, visionary legend and great politician,Kokan (Raigad) Jewel He was former MLA shri Ashokdada sable

He born on 7 june 1944 in mangaon . This college was established in the year 2019 . It was the dream of Hon'b Ashokdada sable ji

He was an academician who wanted everyone to be educated for individual growth and social progress. Th e society, which started as a planting turned into a huge tree today. The Law College is being named after such a legendary and visionary giant and in the true s ense the college is honoured in all respect.

THE COLLEGE AT A GLANCE

The college is located in prime area of the city. It has its own separate Building with spacious & technologically well equipped class rooms with digital Board & projectors.

The college has an independent building for library & reading. There is separate reading room for girls. The college is having a big & beautiful library with more than 32,000 law books including text books, commentaries, law journals, periodicals, reports of law commissions. The college is also providing computer lab with internet facility in library for the students so as to access required & latest / updated information.

The college conducts & also participate in social activities to retain moral values as well as to practically learn law & its working. The college regularly organizes legal aid camps to provide free legal advice to the poor & needy people. The college associate with Dist. Legal Services Authority Mangaon to organize legal literacy campaign.

The college periodically organizes seminars & guest lectures of renowned personalities from legal fraternity. It also organizes Blood Donation camp every year on 7 june, while celebrating birthday of the real founder of this college Hon Ashokdada sable sir.

The college gives instructions about JMFC exams. It helps the students to prepare & achieve good success in university & competitive exams.

Teacher –guardian scheme is also introduced through which the parents are given regular feedback about their word's performance in the class tests, attendance etc. Parents Meetings are organized regularly.

The college has different games and sports facilities such as Badminton, Basketball, Volley ball, Cricket, Net Ball etc. and a well equipped Gymnasium.

The qualified & experienced teaching staff members are striving hard to give their best.
