    Principal Message

      Mangaon Shikshan Prasark Mandal’s ASHOKDADA SABLE LAW COLLEGE is one of the premiere institutes in Kokan (raigad) region of Maharashtra. Hon. Adv.Rajiv Ashok sable president and the Founder Member of Mangaon Shikshan Prasark Mandal’s took initiative in establishing various colleges in this region and Law college was one of the college established for imparting legal education to the poor, needy and downtrodden people. Today Ashokdada sable Law College stands with a bright future. Here at ASHOKDADA SABLE LAW COLLEGE learning makes the individual socially relevant, technically compe¬tent and professionally sound. The college has a vision to promote excellence in the field of legal education. Here at Ashokdada sable law College the law course have been developed in consultation with the legal professionals and other key stake holders to ensure students gain practical, work-ready skills, underpinned by a strong foundation of law, legal theory and professional training.
