Principal Message

    Mangaon Shikshan Prasark Mandal’s ASHOKDADA SABLE LAW COLLEGE is one of the premiere institutes in Kokan (raigad) region of Maharashtra. Hon. Adv.Rajiv Ashok sable president and the Founder Member of Mangaon Shikshan Prasark Mandal’s took initiative in establishing various colleges in this region and Law college was one of the college established for imparting legal education to the poor, needy and downtrodden people. Today Ashokdada sable Law College stands with a bright future. Here at ASHOKDADA SABLE LAW COLLEGE learning makes the individual socially relevant, technically compe¬tent and professionally sound. The college has a vision to promote excellence in the field of legal education. Here at Ashokdada sable law College the law course have been developed in consultation with the legal professionals and other key stake holders to ensure students gain practical, work-ready skills, underpinned by a strong foundation of law, legal theory and professional training.


    Critical thinking, ethics, global outlook, self management are some of the characteristics and skills which are developed in students. The faculty of law regularly hosts networking events and career seminars and actively consults with profes¬sional bodies on emerging trends and needs in legal profession. The faculty of Laws excellent relationships with the industry and community are facilitated through its institutes and alumni's which support develops and pro¬motes activities in existing or emerging fields of strategic relevance and importance. We at ASHOKDADA SABLE LAW COLLEGE are offering three years and five year degree courses in Law LL.B


    Faculty of the college adopts a variety of teaching methods to make the teaching-learning experience effective and to achieve the desired learning outcomes. Some of these methods are enumerated below:

    • Lecture cum Discussion method.
    • Power Point presentations and live discussions on current issues.
    • Case Studies
    • Presentations, industry visits, field visits, educational tours
    • Discussions and debates
    • Practical training by visiting Courts Jails, Advocate Chamber, etc.Assignment that require team based learning
    • Experiential training for Moot Court and other legal competition.
    • Guest Lectures from industry experts, Tax Consultants, Cyber Crime experts, Vocational training and soft skill experts etc.
    • Sports and cultural activities.
